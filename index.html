10 Ways To Fix Traffic Congestion

Traffic Congestion

Traffic congestion is a situation where the road capacity is limited and there are too many vehicles on that road. Traffic congestion makes most vehicles move very slowly and sometimes they don't even move at all. Here are some ways to fix this problem:

1. Less/No Delays In Schedule (Public Transportation)

To make people trust and rely on public transportation such as buses, trains and planes, we have decided to strict the schedule for public transportation. If the driver or the railroad engineer creates a delay in schedule, they will have to pay of a fine of $1 for every minute of delay.

2. Free Internet

Internet or Wi-Fi is very important for many people nowadays. So we should supply public transportation with free internet.

3. Advertisement

One of the best ways to make people want to use transportation is by using advertisements on public transportation such as posters.

4. Fixed Roads

People will often avoid potholes on roads and cause traffic congestion so people can drive easier and without worrying about their cars.

5. Make Larger Roads

Making the roads larger reduce traffic congestion by increasing the volume of the road. Not only that, it could also increase the safety on roads.

6. Expansion of Railway Networks

As the population in the cities grow, the demand of the transportation will also increase. To respond to that, the railway networks will expand so it could efficiently transport many people from place to place.

7. Bridge For Cycling And Walking

With this idea, people with cars can travel faster without worrying about people on the road and cyclists.

8. Cycling Lane In Public Roads

If we separate bicycles with bigger vehicles, cyclists will feel much safer and there will be less accidents.
